Salary Research

Knowing your market value is essential for negotiating a fair salary and benefits package. Use these resources and tips to empower your next negotiation.

How to Research Salaries

  • Identify your job title and relevant experience level.
  • Consider your location, industry, and company size.
  • Use multiple sources to get a well-rounded view.
  • Look for recent data (within the last 1-2 years).
  • Take note of additional compensation (bonuses, equity, benefits).

Tips for Negotiating Your Offer

  • Know your worth and be ready to share your research.
  • Consider the full compensation package, not just base salary.
  • Practice your negotiation conversation with a friend or mentor.
  • Be confident, but also collaborative and open to discussion.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for time to consider the offer.
  • Express gratitude, regardless of the outcome.
