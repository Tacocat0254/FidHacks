Interview Prep

Preparation is the key to interview success. Use these resources and tips to boost your confidence and perform your best.

Common Interview Questions

  • Tell me about yourself.
  • Why do you want this job?
  • What are your strengths and weaknesses?
  • Describe a challenge you faced and how you handled it.
  • Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
  • Why should we hire you?
  • Tell me about a time you worked on a team.
  • Describe a time you failed and what you learned.
  • Technical/role-specific questions (e.g., coding, case studies, portfolio review)

Tips for Interview Success

  • Research the company and role in advance.
  • Practice your answers out loud, ideally with a friend or mentor.
  • Prepare questions to ask the interviewer.
  • Dress appropriately for the company culture.
  • Arrive (or log in) early and test your technology if it's a virtual interview.
  • Be honest about your experience and focus on your ability to learn.
  • Send a thank you note after the interview.
